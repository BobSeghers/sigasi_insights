<p>Tycho, the new Maven base Eclipse build system, was <a> recently accepted</a> as an <a href="http://www.eclipse.org/tycho/" class="elf-external elf-icon">Eclipse project</a>. In the latest, <a href="http://dev.eclipse.org/mhonarc/lists/tycho-user/msg00102.html" class="elf-external elf-icon">0.12.0</a> release, the namespace was updated from <em>org.sonatype.tycho</em> to <em>org.eclipse.tycho</em>. This results in lots of changes to your pom.xml files.</p><p>I used following script to update; it might save you some time too:</p><p><div class="geshifilter"><pre class="bash geshifilter-bash" style="font-family:monospace;"><span style="color: #000000; font-weight: bold;">for</span> i <span style="color: #000000; font-weight: bold;">in</span> pom.xml <span style="color: #000000; font-weight: bold;">*/</span>pom.xml <span style="color: #000000; font-weight: bold;">do</span>  <span style="color: #c20cb9; font-weight: bold;">sed</span> <span style="color: #660033;">-i</span> <span style="color: #660033;">-e</span> <span style="color: #ff0000;">'s/org.sonatype.tycho/org.eclipse.tycho/g'</span> <span style="color: #007800;">$i</span>;  <span style="color: #c20cb9; font-weight: bold;">sed</span> <span style="color: #660033;">-i</span> <span style="color: #660033;">-e</span> <span style="color: #ff0000;">'s/maven-osgi-compiler-plugin/tycho-compiler-plugin/g'</span> <span style="color: #007800;">$i</span>;  <span style="color: #c20cb9; font-weight: bold;">sed</span> <span style="color: #660033;">-i</span> <span style="color: #660033;">-e</span> <span style="color: #ff0000;">'s/maven-osgi-packaging-plugin/tycho-packaging-plugin/g'</span> <span style="color: #007800;">$i</span>;  <span style="color: #c20cb9; font-weight: bold;">sed</span> <span style="color: #660033;">-i</span> <span style="color: #660033;">-e</span> <span style="color: #ff0000;">'s/maven-osgi-source-plugin/tycho-source-plugin/g'</span> <span style="color: #007800;">$i</span>;  <span style="color: #c20cb9; font-weight: bold;">sed</span> <span style="color: #660033;">-i</span> <span style="color: #660033;">-e</span> <span style="color: #ff0000;">'s/maven-osgi-test-plugin/tycho-surefire-plugin/g'</span> <span style="color: #007800;">$i</span>;  <span style="color: #c20cb9; font-weight: bold;">sed</span> <span style="color: #660033;">-i</span> <span style="color: #660033;">-e</span> <span style="color: #ff0000;">'s/maven-tycho-plugin/tycho-pomgenerator-plugin/g'</span> <span style="color: #007800;">$i</span>;  <span style="color: #c20cb9; font-weight: bold;">sed</span> <span style="color: #660033;">-i</span> <span style="color: #660033;">-e</span> <span style="color: #ff0000;">'s$&lt;tycho-version&gt;0.11.0&lt;/tycho-version&gt;$&lt;tycho-version&gt;0.12.0&lt;/tycho-version&gt;$g'</span> <span style="color: #007800;">$i</span>; <span style="color: #000000; font-weight: bold;">done</span></pre></div></p><p>If your build is not working because Maven can not download Tycho 0.12.0 from your Maven mirror. Run:<br/><div class="geshifilter"><pre class="bash geshifilter-bash" style="font-family:monospace;">mvn <span style="color: #660033;">-U</span> clean</pre></div><br/>This <a href="http://dev.eclipse.org/mhonarc/lists/tycho-user/msg00130.html" class="elf-external elf-icon">forces a check for updated releases</a> and snapshots on remote repositories and makes sure the update is found.</p><p>Hendrik.</p>  </div>
